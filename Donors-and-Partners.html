Our Donors & Partners

RAHAMA is proud to work with a diverse range of organizations and individuals who share our commitment to empowering girls, women, and communities. At Rahama Girl Child and Women Empowerment Initiative, our impact is strengthened and amplified through the generous support of our donors and the collaborative spirit of our partners. We are deeply grateful to international agencies, government bodies, community organizations, and local stakeholders who share our vision of empowering vulnerable women, youth, and children across Nigeria. Together, we leverage resources, expertise, and innovative solutions to create lasting change in education, health, economic empowerment, and human rights. Our partnerships with esteemed organizations such as PEPFAR, UNITAID, Médecins Du Monde, Treatment Action Group, and many others are vital to expanding our reach and effectiveness. We remain committed to transparency, accountability, and mutual respect as we work hand in hand with our donors and partners to build healthier, more equitable communities.
