Our Mission

Our mission is to advocate, promote quality education, capacity building programs, economic empowerment and health interventions to vulnerable women, youths and deprived children in Nigeria through Research, Advocacy and Innovation.

What We Do

Our work focuses on three interconnected pillars that address the root causes of vulnerability.

Education & Girl-Child Protection

Keeping girls in school through advocacy, scholarships, and community engagement.

Health & Disease Prevention

Strengthening TB, HIV, and maternal health outcomes through community-led interventions.

Advocacy & Systems Strengthening

Working with government and communities to improve policies and service delivery.
