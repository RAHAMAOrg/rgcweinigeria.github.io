Welcome to RAHAMA

Empowering girls and women across Northern Nigeria

About Us

RAHAMA Girl Child and Women Empowerment Initiative is a nonprofit empowering adolescent girls and women through education, skills acquisition, and rights protection.

Our Projects

  • Girls/Women Empowerment Project: Empowering 5,000 women through skill acquisition (534 already earning).
  • Education Project: Keeping children in school, currently supporting 4 children (2 GBV survivors, 1 disabled).
  • Prisoners Rehabilitation: Empowering 7,000 female prisoners and juveniles (100 already supported).
  • Community Paralegals: Trained 56 community heads in Kano on conflict mitigation.
  • Maternal and Child Health: Sensitization and healthcare worker training in 4 communities.
  • Anti-Rape Campaign: Legal and psychosocial support to child abuse and rape survivors.
  • Social Awareness Project: Over 300 beneficiaries reached in Kano and Bauchi through sensitization and counselling.

Active Projects

  • ASCENT DR-TB Project: Awareness and advocacy for BPaL/M DR-TB treatment in Kano.
  • AYP Project: Community-led monitoring and HIV education for adolescents.
  • CBA Zango: Health literacy and community feedback to improve healthcare in Katsina.
  • TPT Project: Community awareness and acceptability study of 1HP TB prevention treatment in Kano.
