Our Mission

Our mission is to advocate, promote quality education, capacity building programs, economic empowerment and health interventions to vulnerable women, youths and deprived children in Nigeria through Research, Advocacy and Innovation.

What We Do

Our work focuses on four interconnected pillars that address the root causes of vulnerability.

Education & Girl-Child Protection

Keeping girls in school through advocacy, scholarships, and community engagement.

Health & Disease Prevention

Strengthening TB, HIV, and maternal health outcomes through community-led interventions.

Economic Empowerment

Supporting women and youths with skills, livelihoods, and income opportunities.

Advocacy & Systems Strengthening

Working with government and communities to improve policies and service delivery.

Impact Stories from the Community

"I and almost everyone around me gave up on life except my father. And because of that, I felt hopeful and tried to get better. Alhamdulillah, I am now better."

— Khadija

TB Survivor, Kano State

Khadija's journey reminds us of the power of family support, community empathy, and never giving up. Through RAHAMA's TB prevention and treatment programs, she found hope and healing.
