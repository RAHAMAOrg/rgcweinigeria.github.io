About Us

RAHAMA is proud to work with a diverse range of organizations and individuals who share our commitment to empowering girls, women, and communities. At Rahama Girl Child and Women Empowerment Initiative, our impact is strengthened and amplified through the generous support of our donors and the collaborative spirit of our partners.

About RAHAMA

Founded on 4th June 2018, RAHAMA Girl Child & Women Empowerment Initiative is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering vulnerable women, youth, and children in Nigeria. We address systemic barriers that limit access to education, health, and economic opportunities through community-led programs, advocacy, and strategic partnerships.

Vision

A Nigerian society with equal access to quality education, health, and fundamental human rights.

Mission

To advocate, promote and implement quality education, capacity building, health interventions, and economic empowerment programs for women, youth, and deprived children through research, advocacy, and innovation.

Our Story

RAHAMA was established by a group of young activists who saw the urgent need to support girls and women facing educational exclusion, limited health access, and socioeconomic vulnerability.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We serve with empathy and respect for human dignity.
  • Accountability: We uphold transparency and responsibility in all our work.
  • Equity: We strive for fairness in access to opportunities and outcomes.
  • Innovation: We adopt creative solutions to complex challenges.

How We Work

Our approach combines community consultation, evidence-based planning, and collaborative partnerships with government agencies, civil society, and local stakeholders.
