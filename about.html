Rahama Girl Child and Women Empowerment Initiative (RAHAMA) is a non-profit organization established by a group of young activists dedicated to promoting equality and human rights across Nigeria. Our mission is to support and empower vulnerable women, youth, and children.

RAHAMA responds to critical issues affecting girls and women such as limited access to education and health services, early marriage, sexual and gender-based violence, and systemic human rights violations.

Through deep community engagement, including baseline surveys and dialogue with local leaders, we identify challenges and implement impactful solutions. We partner with government bodies, international NGOs, community-based organizations, and grassroots groups to ensure sustainable development and empowerment.

Vision Statement: A Nigerian Society with Equal Access to Quality Education, Health and Fundamental Human rights.

Mission Statement: Our Mission is to advocate, promote quality education, capacity building programs, economic empowerment and health interventions to vulnerable women, youths and deprived children in Nigeria through Research, Advocacy and Innovation.

Main Goal: To achieve an egalitarian society with equal rights and opportunities.
