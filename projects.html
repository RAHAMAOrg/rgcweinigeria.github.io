Active Projects

At RAHAMA Girl Child and Women Empowerment Initiative, our journey has been marked by steadfast commitment and unwavering passion to improve the health and wellbeing of Nigeria's most vulnerable populations. Through strategic partnerships with organizations like PEPFAR, Unitaid, Médecins Du Monde, and the Obama Foundation, we implement evidence-based programs that create lasting change in education, health, and economic empowerment.

Active

ASCENT DR-TB Project

Oct 2024 - Jul 2025 Kano State
Health TB Prevention

Improving access to shorter, safer drug-resistant TB treatment regimens (BPaL/M) through community-led advocacy and policy influence in Kano State.

Key Impact:
  • Accelerating national scale-up of innovative DR-TB treatments
  • Community-led monitoring for treatment quality
  • Policy advocacy for wider BPaL/M availability
Funded by: Unitaid through Treatment Action Group (TAG)

Girls' Education & Protection Program (GEPP)
