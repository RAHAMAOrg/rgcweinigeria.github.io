Our Projects

RAHAMA runs impactful projects to empower women, girls, and communities through education, health, legal support, and advocacy.

ASCENT DR-TB Project

Improving access to safer, shorter DR-TB regimens through community advocacy and healthcare worker training in Kano State.

AYP Project

Enhancing community-led monitoring and virtual engagement for HIV services among Adolescent & Young People in Kano State.

CBA Zango Project

Improving health literacy, service uptake, and community feedback systems in Katsina State.

TPT Project

Building community demand for 1HP TB prevention regimen among high-risk populations in Kano State.

Girls/Women Empowerment Project

Skill acquisition and entrepreneurship training for less privileged women and girls in Kano State and beyond.

Education Project

Providing school fees, uniforms, and books for children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Prisoners & Juvenile Rehabilitation Project

Empowering and supporting female prisoners and juvenile offenders through skills and legal aid.

Community Paralegal & ADR Project

Training community leaders in legal support and conflict resolution in Kano and Jigawa States.

Maternal & Child Health Project

Increasing maternal and child health service uptake and training health workers in early referral.

Action Against Child Abuse & Rape

Providing legal, health, and counseling support to survivors while raising awareness in schools.

Social Awareness Project

Creating awareness on social issues, counseling, and community outreach in Kano and Bauchi States.
